The Problem

Why we started

Research and Development is the engine of progress, yet the tools R&D teams use have remained stuck in the past. Researchers are drowning in technical content, spending long nights skimming through literature instead of innovating.

We saw three critical failures in the market: generic chatbots that hallucinate on scientific data, a massive delay in project milestones due to manual literature reviews, and a fragmented workflow where summaries never quite connect to experiment plans. We built yan-2 to solve this.

"Our goal is to ensure no researcher ever has to waste budget on a misdirected experiment caused by hallucinated AI data."
