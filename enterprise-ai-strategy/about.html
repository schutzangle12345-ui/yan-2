Our Story

Founded to Solve the Enterprise AI Paradox

For years, enterprise executives have been caught between two worlds: vendors promising futuristic AI utopias and internal teams running fragmented pilots that never scale. This gap results in skepticism, wasted budgets, and missed market opportunities.

yan-2 was founded to provide a third way. We are a specialized consulting agency focused exclusively on the strategic implementation of Enterprise AI. We don't just "do AI"—we engineer the roadmap, governance, and business cases that make AI a sustainable competitive advantage.

"Our mission is to empower CTOs and CAIOs with the structured criteria and KPI frameworks needed to lead the AI transformation with confidence."
Core Values

The Principles Driving Our Strategy

Pragmatic 90-Day Value

We reject "boiling the ocean." Every roadmap we build is designed to show measurable results within the first quarter to build stakeholder trust.

Outcome-First Thinking

We start with the P&L and OKRs, then work backward to the technology. If an AI initiative doesn't move a key metric, it shouldn't be on the roadmap.

Strategic Neutrality

We are vendor-agnostic. Our "Build vs Buy vs Partner" frameworks are designed for your specific scale, risk appetite, and internal capabilities.
